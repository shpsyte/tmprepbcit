Christ Church Cathedral in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, is the second cathedral church of the Anglican Diocese of New Westminster of the Anglican Church of Canada. A place of worship in Greater Vancouver, the cathedral is located at 690 Burrard Street on the north-east corner of West Georgia Street and Burrard Street, directly across from the Fairmont Hotel Vancouver.

The Cathedral’s motto is Open Doors, Open Hearts, Open Minds. At Christ Church Cathedral we hope that by opening our doors, our hearts and our minds we might help everyone who comes to us enter into a deeper relationship with God, through Christ. We also work to make God’s love real and present to this city.

Worship with us

On Sundays we offer four services: 8am, 10:30am, 5:30pm and 8pm. The pastoral team are always happy to greet new faces (and old friends) and to answer any questions. Our doors are also open Monday to Friday from 10am to 4pm. All are welcome to stop by to spend some time in quiet prayer and to worship at our weekday 12:10pm Eucharist.

Dive Deeper


Growing your faith is an ongoing journey, one that requires support from a wider faith community. The Cathedral has several Christian formation groups dedicated to strengthening the core values of the Christian faith in a variety of ways, and to helping community members be attentive to what God may be asking of them. Sign up for our weekly newsletter to learn more about the various formation opportunities available at the Cathedral.

Give Back


Volunteer your time cooking and sharing meals through the Cathedral’s Maundy Café. Get involved with the Women’s Brunch or the Men’s Breakfast. Food and hospitality are at the heart of Cathedral life and a vibrant Christian faith.

LGBT - A HISTORY OF ADVOCACY

Christ Church Anglican Cathedral in Vancouver, British Columbia has been advocating for the full inclusion of all God’s people for some time. For over four decades the Cathedral community has extended a wide welcome to LGBTQIA/2S people, their families, friends and allies.
